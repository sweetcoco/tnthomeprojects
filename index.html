Decks

We provide general repair to existing decks and new construction of decks. When capturing the design of your deck we take into consideration that this is an extension of your home. Will your deck be a roof top deck or an observation deck for a beautiful view? Will your deck be used for large family cookouts and pool parties which may entail bench seating as well? Will your deck be a 2 tier deck serving 2 purposes, both for viewing and family gatherings? Maybe you’re looking for your deck to be used for quite time, to wind down in the evenings with a small fire pit? We understand not everyone is looking for a square or rectangle deck and we can take it to the next level! Maybe you’re just looking to enhance your exsisting dull boring deck and want to add a designed railing or a pergola canopy to spruce things up. Whatever your goal is, we are excited to help you achieve it!
