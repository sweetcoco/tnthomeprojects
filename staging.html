Staging

A few reasons to stage:

  • It helps to sell your home faster

  • It will help you to get the highest price for your home

  • It doesn't really cost to stage, it PAYS!


Though staging won’t make a home sell for more than it’s worth, it will MAKE A STRONG IMPRESSION! In today’s challenging market you need to set your home apart. We understand the object is to sell quickly and make more money. On average, 73% of staged homes sell faster than those on the market that are not staged. It’s also estimated that sellers make 7% - 10% more when a home is staged. I think we all would agree a home shows better with furniture, wall art, area rugs, lamps and other accessories or maybe just to neutralize and update existing furnishings.

Without an attractive presence whether it is in person or digital, your home may be passed over at first glance and we only get one chance to make that first impression! Staging is a good way to ensure your home pops. The good news is there are many things you can do to spruce up the look of your home without shelling out a lot of money. Many times, people can “shop at home” to create that pleasing and balanced environment.


Interior Design


  • Did you just move into a new home that needs YOUR personality?

  • Do you have a room that needs a facelift?

  • Is your home outdated?

  • What about a room that is just missing something to make it pop?

  • Are you just too busy and need someone that can finish that room for you, that you’ve been planning on working on for months?


Interior design consists of several different moving parts. We are experienced with space planning, furniture design, lighting and architecture enhancements. There are several objectives to consider when thinking of new looks for your home. We will assist you with style and the theme of your room or home because it needs to represent you and your family. In order for a room to not feel lopsided and feel comfortable, it needs to have the right balance. Mixing colors, patterns and textures isn’t only fun, it’s necessary!

We will take the time to understand what you are looking for and how to combine that with style and functionality.
